story:
  title: "A Family Finally Able to Rest"
  content: |
    I was contacted by two sisters who had been caring for their mother for a
    long time. They were devoted to her, but like many families, their own needs
    had quietly been put aside, and they hadn't had time away together with
    their families for years.

    They reached out as they wanted to take a holiday, but needed to feel
    completely confident that their mum would be supported, safe, and
    comfortable in their absence.

    I took the time to understand their mother's needs and routines, as well as
    what would give the family genuine peace of mind — something I always
    prioritise, as care is not just about the individual, but those who love
    them too.

    I carefully selected carers who would provide both practical support and
    companionship, ensuring their mum felt comfortable and at ease in familiar
    surroundings. I also introduced regular WhatsApp updates, offering
    reassurance and allowing the family to stay connected without worry.

    Knowing their mum was happy and well cared for meant they were able to relax
    and enjoy their holiday together, something they hadn't been able to do for
    a very long time.

    When they returned, their gratitude was deeply felt. Most importantly, they
    had the chance to rest and spend quality time with their own families,
    knowing everything at home was being looked after.

    — Charmaine Jennings
