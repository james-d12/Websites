One-to-One Chaperone Care

St Catherine’s Chaperone Carers provide calm, reliable support for medical appointments, family gatherings, and social
events. For people who are naturally sociable and recently moved into a care home, this service is highly valuable due
to the importance of keeping active and engaging with others. If your loved one has reduced mobility, anxiety, or
cognitive challenges, these outings can feel overwhelming. Our Carers ensure they feel safe, supported, and confident
throughout.

We take time to understand your loved one’s routines and preferences, ensuring every outing is well planned and
unhurried. During medical appointments, carers relay outcomes, specialist advice, and can collect prescriptions if
needed. Immediate assistance for personal care is always provided with dignity and discretion at all times.

This service relieves pressure on families, removing the stress of scheduling or attending appointments themselves.
Families receive regular feedback, giving reassurance and peace of mind.

Through consistency, companionship, and practical support, our Chaperone Carers help individuals remain independent,
socially connected, and supported while families feel informed, reassured, and never alone.
