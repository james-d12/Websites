Hello,

With nearly twenty years of experience in the care industry, I work with one simple belief at heart: care should be delivered with the same love, respect,
and dedication we would give our own family.

My passion for care comes from personal experience. Watching my grandfather live with dementia was one of the most heartbreaking experiences my family
faced. For months, we gently explained every twenty minutes that his wife of
seventy two years had passed away, each time witnessing the shock, physical
pain, and deep grief as though he were hearing it for the first time. Without
the carers he trusted and who we carefully chose, our family would not have
been able to cope or support him as he deserved.

That experience is why St Catherine’s exists today, to give something
meaningful back to families walking a similar path.

We support care homes, elderly clients and individuals needing assistance at
home. Our services include companionship, chaperoning to appointments,
keeping people safe for those at risk of falls, overnight care, post-operative support, personal assistance, and specialist one-to-one dementia care.

Every service we provide is deeply personal, delivered with dignity and compassion, and thoughtfully tailored to honour the unique needs of each
loved one.

Our Carers and Personal Assistants are highly skilled, with years of experience who will create a calm environment and we will ensure continuity which helps with a trusted connection. This reduces stress, disruption and frustration which makes an enormous difference to quality of life.

Carers and Personal Assistants are carefully matched to each client. However,
if for any reason, the fit is not quite right, we will continue to work with
you until it is. We work closely with families to ensure consistency, calm,
and comfort.

Our care does not stop at placement. We can provide regular feedback, for ongoing support, hourly, daily, or weekly, depending on what suits you best. Whether you are nearby or overseas, we strive to make care seamless and reassuring. Peace of mind and rest is also highly important for families, so
you can trust us to take the reins for as long as you need.

At St Catherine’s, care is not just a service, it is a promise.

Warmest regards,
Charmaine Jennings
