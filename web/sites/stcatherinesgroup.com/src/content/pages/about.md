Welcome to St Catherine’s Care,

“Care you can trust and every hour we donate to BRACE Research to fight dementia for a brighter future”

My name is Charmaine and I have worked in the care industry for over a decade. Throughout my career, I have specialised
in providing one-to-one carers and personal assistant support, always with one clear belief at heart: care should be
delivered with the same love, respect, and dedication as if it were for our own family.

At St Catherine’s, we support elderly clients and individuals who need help or assistance at home or in care homes for a
variety of reasons. This may include companionship, accompaniment to appointments, reassurance and safety for those at
risk of falls, watching over at night, additional support following an operation, personal assistance, butler services,
or specialist one-to-one dementia care. Every service we provide is tailored, personal, and delivered with dignity.

My passion for care comes from personal experience. Watching my grandfather live with dementia was one of the most
heartbreaking experiences my family has faced. For months, we had to gently explain every twenty minutes that his wife
of 72 years had passed away, each time witnessing him experience the shock, physical pain, and devastating grief as
though he were hearing the news for the very first time. Without the carers he trusted and we chose, our family would
not have been able to cope or support him in the way he deserved. That experience is the reason why St Catherine’s was
created, to give something meaningful back to families walking a similar path.

St Catherine’s carers, are fully trained, highly skilled, and bring years of experience from many walks of life. A calm
environment, consistency, joy, and reduced stress can make an enormous difference to quality of life, and we take this
responsibility seriously.

I work closely with families to ensure we find the right match for your loved one. If, for any reason, the fit is not
quite right, we will continue working together until we do. Our care does not stop at placement. We provide regular
feedback, whether hourly, daily, or weekly, depending on what suits you best. Peace of mind and rest is also highly
important for families. Families we support are based in the UK and worldwide, and we are proud to make care feel
seamless and reassuring wherever you are.

Dementia touches one in three families, whether personally or through someone we love. In support of ongoing research,
St Catherine’s donates 25p for every hour of care provided to BRACE Research, a charity dedicated solely to funding
vital dementia research. Their work focuses on understanding the brain, improving early diagnosis, and developing new
methods of prevention, treatment, and ultimately, a cure.

At St Catherine’s, care is not just a service, it is a promise. We are a company built on compassion, integrity, and
dedication, striving to create a brighter future for those in our communities. We believe that no one should suffer in
silence. By supporting each other, sharing understanding, and providing care with heart, we help families feel
supported, connected, and never alone. St Catherine’s is more than care, we are a community committed to hope, dignity,
and a life of joy for every individual we serve.
